Bby penguino

There once was a bby penguino, Who liked to watch weed grow. He had black curly hair, But didn't like to stare At the day-old halloumi That he left in his hand, And had mixed with sand, After a night out, Having eaten trout. So he ate it And let it sit In his tummy, And thought, ooh yummy! The end.
